Please note that all the labels:

  • GraphicalBar_LeftEnd8x8s_Lvl_Spr:
  • GraphicalBar_Middle8x8s_Lvl_Spr:
  • GraphicalBar_RightEnd8x8s_Lvl_Spr:
Must be prepended with a question mark (example: “?GraphicalBar_LeftEnd8x8s_Lvl_Spr:”) But for tools besides pixi (and possibly GPS, not sure if that is ever going to be used for graphical bar), they SHOULDN'T have the question mark. to avoid potential errors for pixi routines.

For ExGFX90_SP4_HorizontalBar_MegamanHPBar.bin and ExGFX90_SP4_VerticalBar_MegamanHPBar.bin:

  1. Open StatusBarSettings.asm in the defines folder and set these defines: 
    		!Default_LeftPieces                  = 2             ;\These will by default, set the RAM for the pieces for each section
		!Default_MiddlePieces                = 4             ;|
		!Default_RightPieces                 = 2             ;/
  2. Open GraphicalBarConvertToTile.asm and at label GraphicalBar_LeftEnd8x8s_Lvl_Spr, replace the 3 tables with this: 
    				GraphicalBar_LeftEnd8x8s_Lvl_Spr:
				;Left end fill amount tile numbers:
				db $85		;>Fill amount/index: $00 ($00 out of $02)
				db $86		;>Fill amount/index: $01 ($01 out of $02)
				db $87		;>Fill amount/index: $02 ($02 out of $02)
				GraphicalBar_Middle8x8s_Lvl_Spr:
				;Middle fill amount tile numbers
				db $96		;>Fill amount/index: $00 ($00 out of $04)
				db $97		;>Fill amount/index: $01 ($01 out of $04)
				db $8A		;>Fill amount/index: $02 ($02 out of $04)
				db $8B		;>Fill amount/index: $03 ($03 out of $04)
				db $9A		;>Fill amount/index: $04 ($04 out of $04)
				GraphicalBar_RightEnd8x8s_Lvl_Spr:
				;Right end fill amount tile numbers:
